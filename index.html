Research Questions

My research question is based on explosives. I am taking a look on 3 different types of explosives, TNT, ANFO, and PENT. I decided to measure how many of a certain atom is in 5 US tons of the three different explosives, nitrogen in TNT, hydrogen in ANFO and oxygen in PENT. I first converted US tons to grams, which is 4,538,925 grams to 1 US ton. For ANFO, I had to determine the chemical compound for fuel oil and from there determine the 94.3% Ammonium Nitrate and the 5.7% Fuel Oil combination.


Assumptions

I assumed that the fuel oil would be Diesel Fuel with a chemical compound of 12 Carbon and 23 Hydrogen (average courtesy of Google). These assumptions could possibly affect the accuracy of the calculations because diesel fuel has different chemical formulas and the formula I have is averaged.
